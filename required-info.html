Informations Required for ZHNET LIR Services:


1. ASN Registration: Individual Organization (Neutral Person)

1.1 In RIPE NCC Service Area:
· Identify Document (Passport / ID Card)
1.2 Out of RIPE NCC Service Area:
· Identify Document (Passport / ID Card)
· Proof of network event in RIPE NCC Service Area (Invoice with Europe Upstream Hosting Provider such as Vultr Monthtly Invoice / iFog Product Invoice) Attention: Proof of Documents have to registed with your individual Person


2. ASN Registration: Business Organization

2.1 In RIPE NCC Service Area:
· Business License / Registry Certificate Issued by Government
2.2 Out of RIPE NCC Service Area:
· Business License / Registry Certificate Issued by Government
· Proof of network event in RIPE NCC Service Area (Invoice with Europe Upstream Hosting Provider such as Vultr Monthtly Invoice / iFog Product Invoice) Attention: Proof of Documents have to registed to your organization's information.


3. IP Prefixes Assignment:

3.1 Netname filled: Uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers and "-". Example: ZH-NET / ZHNET / NETZH
3.2 Descr filled: Description of your network (Optional)
3.3 Country filled: the prefixes will use with. (Valid two-letter ISO 3166 country code.)
3.4 admin-c filled: Your Administration Role object in RIPE DATABASE
3.5 tech-c filled: Your Technical Role object in RIPE DATABASE
3.6 abuse-c filled: Your Abuse Contact Role object in RIPE DATABASE (it could same with other role objects but it must have an abuse-mailbox filled init)
